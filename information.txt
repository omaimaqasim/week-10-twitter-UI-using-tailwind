When you apply display: flex to a container, its children become flex items.
By default, flex items have flex-shrink: 1, so when the screen becomes smaller, Flexbox shrinks the children to make them fit inside the container.


If you want to make a div fixed without breaking the layout, put it inside a parent div.
The parent keeps the space in the layout, and the child div stays fixed on the screen.
